1.Project Overview

The Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and profit trends across different categories and regions. The dashboard provides valuable business insights to help improve sales strategies and support data-driven decision-making.

2.Purpose

This dashboard enables users to monitor key business metrics, identify high-performing products and regions, and understand customer purchasing patterns. It helps businesses optimize sales performance and improve profitability.

3.Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Used for creating interactive reports and visualizations.
📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning and transformation.
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures and KPIs.
🔗 Data Modeling – Established relationships between tables for analysis.
📁 File Formats – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard preview.
4.Data Source

Source: Sample E-Commerce Sales Dataset

The dataset contains information related to:

Orders and Sales Amount
Customer Details

Payment Modes
State-wise Transactions
Profit and Quantity Metrics
5.Features & Highlights
🔍 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data, making it difficult to quickly identify profitable products, top customers, and regional sales trends.

Questions such as:

Which states generate the highest revenue?
Which product categories contribute most to sales?
Which payment methods are preferred by customers?
Which sub-categories generate maximum profit?

are difficult to answer using raw data alone.

6. Goal of the Dashboard
Monitor sales and profit KPIs.
Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
Identify top-performing products and regions.
Support business decisions using interactive visualizations.

 8.Dashboard Highlights
Sales by State

Analyzes revenue contribution across different states.

Quantity by Category

Shows the distribution of products sold across categories such as Clothing, Electronics, and Furniture.

Profit by Month

Tracks monthly profit trends to evaluate business performance.

Sales by Customer

Identifies top customers based on purchase amount.

Quantity by Payment Mode

Analyzes customer payment preferences including COD, UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, and EMI.

Profit by Sub-Category

Highlights the most profitable product sub-categories.

Interactive Filters

Quarter and State slicers allow dynamic analysis across different business dimensions.

9. Business Insights
Clothing category contributed the highest sales volume.
Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most preferred payment method.
Maharashtra generated the highest revenue among all states.
Printers and Phones contributed significantly to overall profit.
Monthly profit trends help businesses monitor performance and plan future strategies.
